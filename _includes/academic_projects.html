  • Multi-Response Optimization using GRA, TOPSIS and PCA-TOPSISProf. G.Srinivasa Rao

    B.Tech ProjectApril 2015

                In this work the cutting parameters namely process parameters such as cutting speed, feed rate, depth of cut and tool parameters such as nose radius, rake angle are considered as input factors to study their effects on responses, surface roughness and material removal rate. In this study, the experiments were carrired out as per L18 orthogonal array design. This study highlights the use of Grey Relational Analysis (GRA), Technique for Order Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution (TOPSIS) and Principal Component Analysis based TOPSIS (PCA-TOPSIS). Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) was also used to find out the most influential cutting parameters on to the responses. From the results, It can be observed that PCA-TOPSIS outperformed compared to remaining techniques.
